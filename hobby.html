Hobby & Interests

This page is all about my hobbies and interests. Here, you'll get a close look at what I'm passionate about, from creative pursuits to thrilling adventures.

Downhill & Freeride

Downhill and freeride mountain biking is my passion. It's all about taking my bike down challenging trails, feeling the wind rush past me, and enjoying the thrill of the ride. Whether I'm cruising through the woods, tackling rocky descents, or catching air over jumps, it's all about the excitement, skills, and a deep connection with nature. This hobby keeps me active and in touch with the outdoors, providing a simple yet incredibly satisfying way to spend my time.

PC Building & Gaming

Building gaming PCs and diving into the world of gaming has become a fulfilling and captivating pastime for me. It's the perfect fusion of my technical interests and my love for gaming. Assembling high-performance gaming rigs is like putting together a puzzle of power and precision, and the satisfaction of powering on a self-built machine is hard to beat. But my hobby doesn't stop at the hardware – I'm equally passionate about the gaming experience itself. Whether I'm immersing myself in epic adventures, testing my skills in competitive games, or simply enjoying a casual gaming session, gaming offers me an escape, a challenge, and a world of endless possibilities. Building and gaming with the PCs I create is not just a pastime; it's a source of excitement, creativity, and a way to connect with a global community of fellow gamers.

Traveling

I'm an avid traveler with a passion for exploring the diverse corners of our planet. My journeys have taken me to some of the most enchanting destinations, from the otherworldly landscapes of Iceland to the breathtaking beauty of Switzerland, the ancient wonders of Rome, the vibrant cities of Germany and France, and even the bustling streets of Shenzhen, the heart of electronics. While I've marveled at iconic landmarks, savored delectable cuisine, and immersed myself in local cultures, I've also embarked on a unique adventure, venturing into the intricate world of technology in Shenzhen, where I fixed iPhones and laptops for eager customers.

Coffee Enthusiast

For me, coffee is more than just a beverage; it's a passion that fuses my love for flavors, cultures, and the simple joys of life. Much like an artist with a palette, I explore the intricate world of coffee, where each cup is a canvas painted with unique stories and aromas. In this realm of coffee, I'm not just a consumer, I'm an adventurer. From the vibrant notes of single-origin beans to the comforting embrace of a familiar brew, I've embarked on a flavorful journey that transcends mere sips. It's not only about the coffee itself; it's about the rituals, conversations, and the way coffee connects us to different corners of the world. As I continue to explore the art of coffee, I'm not just a coffee drinker; I'm a connoisseur of culture, a collector of coffee tales, and an enthusiastic explorer of beans, blends, and brewing techniques. Join me on this aromatic adventure where every cup offers a new story, a new flavor, and a new connection to the world.
