Hobby & Interests

This page is all about my hobbies and interests. Here, you'll get a close look at what I'm passionate about, from creative pursuits to thrilling adventures.

Downhill & Freeride

Downhill and freeride mountain biking is my passion. It's all about taking my bike down challenging trails, feeling the wind rush past me, and enjoying the thrill of the ride. Whether I'm cruising through the woods, tackling rocky descents, or catching air over jumps, it's all about the excitement, skills, and a deep connection with nature. This hobby keeps me active and in touch with the outdoors, providing a simple yet incredibly satisfying way to spend my time.

PC Building & Gaming

Building gaming PCs and diving into the world of gaming has become a fulfilling and captivating pastime for me. It's the perfect fusion of my technical interests and my love for gaming. Assembling high-performance gaming rigs is like putting together a puzzle of power and precision, and the satisfaction of powering on a self-built machine is hard to beat. But my hobby doesn't stop at the hardware – I'm equally passionate about the gaming experience itself. Whether I'm immersing myself in epic adventures, testing my skills in competitive games, or simply enjoying a casual gaming session, gaming offers me an escape, a challenge, and a world of endless possibilities. Building and gaming with the PCs I create is not just a pastime; it's a source of excitement, creativity, and a way to connect with a global community of fellow gamers.
